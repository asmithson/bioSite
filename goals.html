Goals +

One of Rose T.'s earliest milestones in beauty school came during curl practice. Styling curls with precision is no small feat — it reflects technique, patience, and an understanding of hair texture.

Rose T. hopes to continue refining these skills to master more complex curl patterns and textured styles.

Braiding someone else's hair is one thing. Braiding your own takes a different kind of skill. Rose T. celebrated a win when successfully braiding their own hair — a test of both dexterity and patience.

Looking ahead, Rose T. is excited to explore more intricate braiding patterns and incorporate them into creative color work and formal styling for events like weddings.

Graduation wasn't just a finish line — it marked courage, growth, and a complete transformation. Rose T. left behind a stable career to follow their passion, and this photo celebrates that achievement.

With a license in hand and real-world experience underway, Rose T. is focused on building a strong foundation in the salon world — with the long-term goal of becoming a color specialist and styling expert for vivid hues and special occasions.

This bouquet quietly honors the support Rose T. received along the way. A quiet reminder that even bold transformations are rarely done alone.
